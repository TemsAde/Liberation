WE ARE LIBERATION

The Redeemed Christian Church of God, Liberation Chapel is an organization under the Redeemed Christian Church of God. Beyond that, we are a family of believers bonded by the love of our Lord Jesus Christ for us and our love for him. A fellowship that stated at Tinubu Square in Lagos Island, Nigeria has today become a growing congreagation of over 500 members at an edifice in Surulere, Lagos. The church, on inception, was led by Pastor Ayo Deinde Isaac, and since then, its leadership has changed hands over the years. By the grace of God and divine leading, the church is currently headed by Pastor Ademola Adeoti.

At Liberation Chapel, Holiness is our watchward, and service, to God and humanity, is our lifestyle. We make it a priority to reach out to those around us, fulfilling the great comission that they might come to the knowledge of God and his only begotten son Jesus Christ, in whom there is true LIBERTY by The Spirit.
